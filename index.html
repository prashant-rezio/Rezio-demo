Read me first!

  1. Open Links in Separate Incognito Windows: Open the provided links side by side in separate incognito tabs or windows. You need to be the buyer and the agent both, else the chat will get stuck without an agent's response whenever needed.
  2. Log in as a Buyer: Once logged in, click on the property listed at the top to start the chat.
  3. Agent Chat: In the agent's chat, click on the button located right under "Ongoing Negotiations."
  4. Ask Questions and Make Offers: As a buyer, feel free to ask questions about the property and make offers.
  5. Submitting Documents: Whenever you are requested for a cheque or a document, simply say, "Here is the cheque," "Here are the documents," or enter a number if an ID is requested.
  6. Refreshing the Chat: If you need to refresh the chat, go to the agent's side and message, "Refresh this chat," then log in again.
  7. Agent Intervention: If a question requires the agent's intervention, check the agent's chat for what the AI is asking for, and respond on behalf of the agent.
